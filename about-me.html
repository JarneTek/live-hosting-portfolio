My Journey

Web Developer & Programmer

My name is Jarne Tekin, I am 21 years old, and my career initially started in sports, where I played football at a decent level for Beerschot for many years. However, I discovered my true passion in the IT sector and decided to change my path. What started as a curiosity for technology grew into a deep fascination with programming and the impact technology can have on the world. I am currently studying to become a programmer at Artevelde University of Applied Sciences, where I am developing my skills in various technologies and programming languages.

What I Want to do

As a junior programmer, my focus lies in web application development and modern frameworks. I'm particularly drawn to React and Angular because of their robust ecosystems and widespread adoption in the industry. These frameworks perfectly align with my goals as they offer:

  • The ability to build scalable and maintainable applications
  • Experience with industry-standard tools and practices
  • Exposure to modern development workflows
  • Opportunities to work in collaborative environments
